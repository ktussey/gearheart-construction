Our Legacy

Over 40 Years of Building Excellence

Founded by Joe Gearheart, Gearheart Construction has been the cornerstone of luxury residential construction in Fort Worth for over four decades. What began as a commitment to exceptional craftsmanship has grown into one of the most respected construction firms in Texas.

Our collaborative approach sets us apart. We work hand-in-hand with architects, engineers, and interior designers to ensure every project reflects our clients' vision while meeting the highest standards of construction quality. This collaborative spirit extends to our long-term relationships with subcontractors and suppliers — partnerships built on mutual respect and shared commitment to excellence.

Many of our clients have been with us since the firm's founding, entrusting us with multiple projects over the decades. These enduring relationships are a testament to the trust and satisfaction that define every Gearheart project.
